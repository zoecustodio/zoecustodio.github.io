User Experience Improvements

Changes I Made for Ux:

  • I made the paragraphs shorter and more manageable to read.
  • Ensured the headings matched with the information presented in the paragraph.
  • Fixed spelling errors and flow of the language, making it more clear and to the point while also using active language.
  • Margin boxes surrond what i believe to be the most important pieces information and there is spacing in the margins to make it easier to read.
  • The links to each page are now onthe side of each page, they no longer crowd the top of the page and the font is bigger and thicker making it easier to read.

What I Learned About User Behavior

I learened that the user will oftne scan in a "F" pattern looking at mainly the menu section, the headlines, and occasionally the middle of the website's paragraph for general information inside the paragraph to see it it aligns with what they are searching for. If it doesnt align then they tend to move on, but the writer also has to be wary that they dont have too large of a text block because that will turn readers off from interacting with the writign unless the absolutely have to.
